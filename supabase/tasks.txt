```bash

NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY=your_supabase_anon_key
```

Supabase Edge Functions Deno par chalte hain, jo ki Cloudflare Workers ke environment se bahut milta-julta hai. Agar aapko future me Cloudflare par move karna ho, to aapko bas yeh karna hoga:
